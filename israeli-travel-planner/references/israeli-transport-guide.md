## Rav-Kav Card
- **Personal**: Requires photo, supports period passes
- **Anonymous**: No ID needed, stored value only
- **Reload**: Train stations, bus terminals, post offices, rav-kav.co.il
- **Passes**: Daily, weekly, monthly, cluster-based

## Shabbat Schedule
- Public transit stops: Friday ~2-3 hours before sundown
- Resumes: Saturday ~1 hour after sundown
- Exceptions: Haifa has limited Shabbat bus service
- Alternatives: Sherut taxis, Gett, Yango
- Yom Kippur: ALL roads closed to vehicles

## National Parks (Rashut HaTeva VeHaGanim)
### Pricing (2025)
- Single entry: Adult 29 NIS, Child 15 NIS
- Kartisiya (6 entries): 110 NIS adult
- Annual pass: ~195 NIS
- Hours: Typically 8:00-17:00 (summer until 18:00)

### Top Parks by Region
**North**: Banias, Tel Dan, Gamla, Korazim
**Center**: Caesarea, Beit Guvrin, Apollonia
**South**: Masada, Ein Gedi, Ein Avdat, Timna
**Dead Sea**: Ein Gedi Nature Reserve, Qumran

## Driving Tips
- Speed limits: 50 km/h urban, 80-90 km/h rural, 110-120 km/h highway
- Toll roads: Highway 6 (electronic), Carmel Tunnels (cash/electronic)
- Parking: Blue-white curb = paid, red-white = no parking
- Gas prices: ~7.50-8.00 NIS/liter
